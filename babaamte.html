Prakash Baba Amte

Social Worker

Prakash Baba Amte is a social worker from Maharashtra, India.
Amte and his wife, Mandakini Amte, were awarded the Magsaysay Award for 'Community Leadership'
in 2008 for their philanthropic work in the form of the Lok Biradari Prakalp amongst the Madia Gonds in the
Gadchiroli district of Maharashtra and the neighbouring states of Telangana and Madhya Pradesh.
